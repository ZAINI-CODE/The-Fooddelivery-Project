Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Browse restaurants by clicking on "Restaurants" from the home page. Select your favorite restaurant, browse their menu, add items to cart, and proceed to checkout. You can track your order after placing it.

What is the minimum order amount?

Minimum order amount varies by restaurant. You can see the minimum order requirement on each restaurant's page. It typically ranges from Rs. 300 to Rs. 1000 depending on the restaurant.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Once an order is confirmed by the restaurant, modifications are not possible. However, you can contact our support team within 5 minutes of placing the order for urgent changes.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including Cash on Delivery, Credit/Debit Cards, and mobile wallets. Payment options are shown during checkout.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payment transactions are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Do you charge any service fee?

We charge a nominal delivery fee which varies by restaurant and distance. The delivery fee is clearly shown before you confirm your order.

Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time varies by restaurant and location, typically ranging from 20 to 50 minutes. Estimated delivery time is shown on each restaurant's page and during checkout.

Can I track my order?

Yes! After placing your order, you'll be redirected to the order tracking page where you can see real-time updates on your order status from preparation to delivery.

What if my order is late?

If your order is significantly delayed beyond the estimated time, please contact our customer support. We'll investigate and may offer compensation depending on the situation.

Do you deliver to my area?

We deliver to major cities across Pakistan including Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Rawalpindi, and Peshawar. Enter your address during checkout to verify if we deliver to your location.

Issues & Support

What if my order is incorrect?

If you receive an incorrect order, please contact us immediately through the support section. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund as per our policy.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via phone at +92 300 1234567 or email at support@fooddelivery.pk. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before the restaurant confirms it. Once confirmed, cancellation may not be possible, but you can contact support for assistance.

Restaurants

How do I become a restaurant partner?

If you're a restaurant owner interested in partnering with us, click on "Sign up to be a restaurant partner" at the top of the page. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.

How do I know if a restaurant is open?

Each restaurant page shows an "Open" or "Closed" badge. You can only order from restaurants that are currently open. Operating hours vary by restaurant.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, please contact our support team at support@fooddelivery.pk or call us at +92 300 1234567. We're here to help!
