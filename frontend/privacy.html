Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 December 2025

1. Overview

This privacy policy explains how this food delivery demo application handles your information. Because this is a demo, we do not collect or store any real payment data.

2. Information We Collect

When you register or log in, we may store your name and email in your browser's local storage for demonstration. This data is not sent to any real server.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

The application may use browser local storage to store your cart, session information, or preferences. This data stays in your browser and can be cleared by you at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Images and icons may be loaded from third-party CDNs or stock photo providers. No personal data is intentionally shared with them by this demo.

5. Contact

For any questions about this demo or its privacy policy, you can contact the project author directly.
