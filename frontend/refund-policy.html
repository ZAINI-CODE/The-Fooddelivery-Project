Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it is confirmed by the restaurant. Once the restaurant has confirmed and started preparing your order, cancellation may not be possible.

1.1 Customer-Initiated Cancellation

If you wish to cancel your order, please do so immediately through the order tracking page or by contacting our support team. Cancellations made within 5 minutes of order placement are typically approved automatically.

1.2 Restaurant-Initiated Cancellation

In rare cases, the restaurant may need to cancel your order due to unavailability of items or other unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, you will receive a full refund to your original payment method.

2. Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • Order cancelled before restaurant confirmation
  • Restaurant cancels the order
  • Incorrect or missing items in your order
  • Food quality issues (spoiled, cold, or not as described)
  • Significant delivery delay beyond the estimated time
  • Order never delivered

3. Refund Process

Refunds are processed based on your original payment method:

3.1 Cash on Delivery

If you paid via Cash on Delivery and are eligible for a refund, the amount will be credited to your account wallet which can be used for future orders, or transferred to your bank account upon request.

3.2 Online Payment (Card/Wallet)

For online payments, refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. The exact time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

3.3 Partial Refunds

In cases of missing or incorrect items, we may offer a partial refund corresponding to the value of the affected items, rather than a full refund.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support within 24 hours of order delivery
  2. Provide your order ID and details of the issue
  3. If applicable, share photos of the incorrect/damaged items
  4. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours
  5. Approved refunds will be processed within the timeframes mentioned above

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Please note that refunds may not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after the order is confirmed
  • Failure to accept delivery (unless due to excessive delay)
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Customer not available at delivery location
  • Refund request made after 24 hours of delivery
  • Partial consumption of the order before complaint

6. Delivery Fee Refunds

Delivery fees are generally non-refundable unless:

  • The order is cancelled before restaurant confirmation
  • The restaurant cancels the order
  • The order is never delivered
  • There is a significant delay caused by our delivery partner

7. Promotional Discounts and Vouchers

If you used a promotional discount or voucher code with your order:

  • Full refunds will include the discounted amount paid
  • Used vouchers/promo codes will be restored to your account if the refund is due to our error
  • One-time use promotional codes may not be restored if the cancellation is customer-initiated

8. Compensation Policy

In addition to refunds, we may offer compensation in the form of credits, vouchers, or discounts for future orders in cases of:

  • Significant delays in delivery
  • Poor food quality (case-by-case basis)
  • Repeated issues with orders
  • Service failures on our part

9. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with our refund decision, you may escalate the matter by contacting our senior support team. We strive to resolve all disputes fairly and promptly.
